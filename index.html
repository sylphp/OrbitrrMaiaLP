about orbitrr

Orbitrr is a creative social platform matching like-minded artists and clients

We aim to empower Artists to make a living through art by matching them with Clients who love their work. This is a revolution against the Ordinary - the rise of a New World built on Passion, Creativity and Identity.

This is the #WorldDominationPlan


How does it work?
Launch
The client launches a project by sending a Mission
Capture
The artists capture the Mission they are interested in
Pitch
The artists pitch to the client by sending draft designs
Connect
The client chooses a design and is connected to the artist
